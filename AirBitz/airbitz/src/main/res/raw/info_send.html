Send Bitcoin

Send bitcoin from the wallet of your choice by clicking the wallet name on the top header.

Scan the QR code of another wallet to obtain the address of the wallet to send funds to.

Tap the [Photos] button to choose a photo containing a QR code you would like to scan.

Tap [Address] to manually type or paste an address to send money to.

If there are bluetooth payment requests near you, they will show up at the bottom. Tap on a request to send to that recipient.

Tap [Transfer] to transfer bitcoin to another wallet in your account. Simply tap the destination wallet name in the popup.
